Discription:-

HTML text formatting is essential for defining the visual appearance and structural importance of content on a webpage.
By using various tags, developers can modify how text is displayed to enhance readability and user engagement. For instance,
some tags make text bold or italicized to emphasize key points, while others are used for specific technical needs, such as
creating subscripts for chemical formulas or superscripts for mathematical exponents.
